Banana Pudding

Description

Definitely a crowd pleaser! It is really easy. I have done it with only the large package of vanilla pudding and also made it with one small package of vanilla and one small package of banana cream. There were no complaints either way from the diehard banana pudding fans in my circle of friends and family.After doing it a few times, I like to line dish with the nilla wafers and banana slices first and then mix up the filling. It seems to all go together faster that way. Maybe it's just in my head. LOL. And for those who like extra crunch, I always have more wafers available.Kudos! to this awesome, easy recipe that everyone I know is crazy about.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups cold skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Â½ (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat cream cheese in a large bowl until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, then pudding mix. Gradually mix in cold milk until smooth, followed by vanilla.
  3. Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
  4. Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish with vanilla wafers.
  5. Arrange sliced bananas evenly on top.
  6. Cover with pudding mixture.
  7. Top with remaining whipped topping.
  8. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving.
